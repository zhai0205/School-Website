Enrollment

Good day! All BNHS students promoted for S.Y. 2023–2024 are automatically enrolled. Incoming Grade 7, transferees, ALS, OHSP, and Balik-Aral (Grades 8–10) must follow the enrollment schedule from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Students who completed online enrollment for Grades 8–10 do not need to come. However, Grade 7 students (including those who did early registration or online enrollment) must attend with a parent/guardian for a reading test.
