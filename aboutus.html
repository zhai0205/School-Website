Our History

Bagumbayan National High School (BNHS) is a public secondary school located in the municipality of Bagumbayan, Taguig City, Philippines. It was established with the aim of providing accessible education to the youth of the area. Over the years, the school has grown and developed into a key educational institution, offering various programs to cater to different student needs. The history of Bagumbayan National High School dates back to the efforts of local leaders and educators who recognized the importance of having a secondary school within the municipality. Initially, classes were held in a temporary facility, but due to the increasing number of enrollees, the school expanded its infrastructure and facilities. Today, BNHS is a well-established institution known for its commitment to providing quality education. It offers various academic tracks and extracurricular activities, ensuring holistic development for its students. It continues to serve the local community, contributing to the academic and personal growth of many young people in the area.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Aims to provide quality, accessible, and relevant education that fosters the development of competent, responsible, and productive citizens. The school is committed to nurturing the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical well-being of its students, helping them become lifelong learners and active participants in nation-building.

Vision

Envisions itself as a leading institution in the region that produces graduates who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in the global community. The school strives to develop individuals who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible and capable of contributing to the growth and development of their communities.
