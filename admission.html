Admission Requirments

Admission Requirements for Junior High School

  • Birth Certificate
  • Report Card/Transcript of Records(Form 137 or Form)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character

Admission Requirments for Senior High School

  • Birth Certificate
  • Report Card/Transcript of Records(Form 137 or Form)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • National Career Assessment Examination (NCAE) Results

For Transferees:

  • Certificate of Transfer/Recommendation Letter
  • Transcript of Records (Form 137)

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our school and admission process.

What are the requirements for Senior High School admission?

Requirements include Form 138 (Report Card), Birth Certificate, Certificate of Good Moral Character, NCAE results, and ID photos. For the complete list, refer to our Admissions page.

Do you offer scholarships for Senior High School students?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on academic performance, sports, and other achievements. For more details, visit the Scholarships section on our website.

What tracks are available for Senior High School?

We offer Academic Tracks (STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS) and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Tracks, as well as Sports and Arts & Design Tracks.

Is there an entrance exam for Senior High School applicants?

Yes, applicants are required to take an entrance exam. Exam schedules are announced during the application period.

Can transferees apply mid-year?

Yes, we accept transferees subject to the availability of slots and submission of the necessary documents.
